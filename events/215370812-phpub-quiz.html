PHPub quiz December 2014

Dec 10
63 attended 5/5 by 6 members

We're trying something different to round off 2014, in December, we'll be hosting a tech-themed pub quiz to celebrate how we've redefined PHPSW this year!

The questions will be techy, but not too serious, geeky, or necessarily, even PHP-orientated. There'll be a bar tab courtesy of GitHub, bar snacks, mince pies and yule logs, and a bunch of great prizes, including but not limited to tickets to PHP UK and jQuery UK conferences, as well as a whole bunch of books, cert vouchers, elePHPants, licenses, t-shirts, vouchers, $325 of Digital Ocean hosting and GitHub, Mandrill, PHP & PHPSW swag.

We're thinking teams of 4, if you don't have enough we'll pair you up, and we'll be hosting at the lovely Smoke & Mirrors bar on Denmark Street (towards The Hatchet).
