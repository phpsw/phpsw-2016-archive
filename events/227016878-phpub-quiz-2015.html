PHPub Quiz 2015 December 2015

Dec 09
49 attended 5/5 by 3 members

For the end of 2015 it's a welcome return to our now annual tech-themed Pub Quiz.

The questions will be techy, but not too serious, geeky, or necessarily, even PHP-orientated. There'll be a bar tab along with some bar snacks and we're in the process of putting a selection of prizes together.

We're thinking teams of 4, if you don't have enough we'll pair you up, and we'll be hosting at the Bristol Ram on Park Street.

We'd like to thank the following people for their prize contributions:

• JetBrains for sponsoring the welcome drinks & 2 PhpStorm licenses

• PHP UK Conference for a conference ticket

• White October Events for a Render conference ticket

• PHP South Coast for a conference ticket

• Nomad PHP for 2 x 6 month subscriptions

• PHP Architect for 2 x 12 month digital subscriptions

• DigitalOcean for a $75, $50 & $25 coupon

• Steve Corona for 2 copies of 'Scaling PHP'

• The Grumpy Programmer (Chris Hartjes) for a copy of 'The Grumpy Programmer's Guide To Building Testable Applications In PHP', 'The Grumpy Programmer's PHPUnit Cookbook' & 'The Grumpy Programmer's Little Book of Hack'

• Phil Sturgeon for 2 copies of 'Build APIs You Won't Hate'

• Chris Cornutt for 2 copies of 'Securing PHP: Core Concepts' and 'Securing PHP: The Usual Suspects'

• Paul M. Jones for a copy of 'Modernizing Legacy Applications In PHP'

• WordCamp London for some swag

• Brightpearl, Meanbee & Ents24 for their ongoing support of PHPSW and providing the buffet, 2 elephpants and 2 Anker PowerCore+ mini chargers.
